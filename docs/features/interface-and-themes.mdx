### About Floorp Design

To customize the interface and design of Floorp, navigate to **Floorp Hub** and click on **Tab & Appearance** in the settings sidebar. The "Interface" settings will be displayed at the top.\
Here, you can modify the design to customize the overall appearance of Floorp.

### Changing the Theme

Navigate to the Add-ons Manager (`about:addons`).\
By default, you can choose from the System theme, Light, Dark, and Floorp Alpenglow.
You can explore even more third-party themes on [Firefox Browser ADD-ONS](https://addons.mozilla.org/).

:::warning
While CSS editing is possible, it is not officially supported. Please do so at your own risk.
:::

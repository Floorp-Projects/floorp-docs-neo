This is a list of developers and contributors to the Floorp project.\
We deeply appreciate the kindness of all the developers, users, and contributors who have contributed.

Please note that some contributions or information updates may not be reflected.

For open-source projects used in the Floorp browser, please see `about:license` in the browser or [here](https://github.com/Floorp-Projects/Floorp?tab=readme-ov-file#-floorp-license-notices-).

Founder of the project, lead developer, and maintainer. Member of Ablaze
